## HotSpot Wizard analysis

Used HotSpot Wizard to identify candidate mutagenesis residues in the GH10/Cex domain (Project A target).

**Column definitions used:**

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Mutable | HotSpot Wizard mutability prediction |
| Non-essential | Whether mutation would preserve protein function |
| Tunnel | Residue controls substrate entry/exit to catalytic site |
| Catalytic pocket | Residue lies in active site and binds substrate directly |

**Selection constraint:** A residue must be (1) non-essential AND (2) in the tunnel and/or catalytic pocket to be a viable target. Catalytic activity depends on both localisation and binding.

## Targets identified

**ASP131** and **ASN172** identified as mutable and meeting the selection constraint.

Neutral/beneficial mutation candidates calculated via homology searching, variability evaluation, statistical analysis, and catalytic residue identification. Compiled into a mutation list for FoldX using the notation `DA131A` (aspartate at position 131 → alanine); semicolon-separated, each entry generates a separate output PDB in FoldX.
