Poured selection plates for conditions (b), (c), and (d) using autoclaved M9 + agar base:

**Condition (b) — M9 + 1% cellobiose + 0.4% glucose + chloramphenicol:**
64.1 ml 5% cellobiose stock + 6.41 ml 20% glucose stock + 320 µl chloramphenicol stock per 250 ml M9+agar. 9× plates made, stored in fridge overnight.

**Condition (c) — M9 + 1% cellobiose + chloramphenicol:**
62.5 ml 5% cellobiose stock + 320 µl chloramphenicol stock per 250 ml M9+agar. 9× plates made, stored in fridge overnight.

**Condition (d) — M9 + 0.4% glucose + chloramphenicol:**
5.1 ml 20% glucose stock + 320 µl chloramphenicol stock per 250 ml M9+agar. 9× plates made, stored in fridge overnight.

*Note: glucose plates marked blue, cellobiose plates marked black, combined marked with both colours. **Kanamycin was not added** to any condition.*
